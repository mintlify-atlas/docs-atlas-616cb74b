# Welcome to EGEL Simulator

EGEL Simulator is a comprehensive desktop application designed to help you prepare for the EGEL (Examen General para el Egreso de la Licenciatura) exam. Built with Electron, React, and TypeScript, it offers a powerful offline-first experience with realistic exam simulations.

## What is EGEL Simulator?

EGEL Simulator provides a complete exam preparation environment that replicates the actual EGEL testing experience. Whether you're studying disciplinary or transversal content areas, the application offers flexible study modes, detailed performance tracking, and immediate feedback to accelerate your learning.

## Who Should Use EGEL Simulator?

EGEL Simulator is designed for:

- **University students** preparing for their EGEL certification exam
- **Recent graduates** who need to pass the EGEL to complete their degree requirements
- **Self-paced learners** who prefer studying on their own schedule without internet dependency
- **Study groups** who want a standardized practice environment across multiple devices

<Tip>
  The offline-first design means you can study anywhere—on campus, at home, or while traveling—without worrying about internet connectivity.
</Tip>
